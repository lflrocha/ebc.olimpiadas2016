Rio 2016: Image database for the press to use free of charge

In partnership with EBC Serviços, Brazil's federal government official website for the Rio 2016 Olympic and Paralympic Games (brasil2016.gov.br) offers a monthly package of raw, aerial and internal video footage of the venues under construction for the mega sporting event in Rio de Janeiro. The high definition footage is available free of charge for the press, as long as the source is mentioned: Brazil’s Ministry of Sport.

The material is organised month by month. In the priority tab, each file has a description of the scenes. If there are any questions about the portrayed facility, see the infographic below with mock-up images of the Olympic arenas. If further clarification is needed, you may get in touch with the brasil2016.gov.br team through the e-mail imprensa@esporte.gov.br or by phoning + 55 61 3217-1875.
